Dragon age: Inquisition 60 FPS mod

This guide helps you uncap cutscenes frame rate.

  1. Open your Origin library, and right-click on Dragon Age Inquisition icon (the cover art).

  2. Select "Game Properties…​".

  3. In the "Command line argument" field, add the following code and then click "OK"

    -GameTime.MaxSimFps 60 -GameTime.ForceSimRate 60+

  4. Launch the game and enjoy

Known issue

This mod causes rubber-band effect when playing multiplayer
